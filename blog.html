Construction Job Management System
- MERN Stack -

I served as the project leader for this university group project, which consisted of four members.
This project facilitates seamless connections between employers and job seekers in the construction industry.
Job seekers can effortlessly find jobs, manage profiles - including approval, pending, and rejected job posts. Employers can hire job seekers, post job vacancies, and explore the platform's features. The Admin Panel serves as a control center for managing users and job postings.

Wedding Planning Services System
- MERN Stack -

The project aims to offer a platform for couples planning their weddings. Key features include secure user registration and login. Service providers, such as florists, photographers, cake designers, and makeup artists, can register by sharing business details and services. Users can enjoy a shopping cart feature to view, modify, and finalize selected services, with automatic cost calculation. This simplifies wedding planning for both couples and vendors.
